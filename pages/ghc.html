Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC)

Want to attend GHC for free?

Every year, the top 15 most active members of Women in Computing (WIC) at KU have the chance to go to the Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC) for free!

GHC, or the Grace Hopper Celebration, is the world's largest gathering of women and non-binary technologists. It's filled with networking, workshops, and talks from industry leaders, all designed to inspire and empower women in tech.

Grace Hopper is an incredible opportunity to connect with top industry leaders, pick up new skills, and get inspired by amazing women in tech. It's the perfect place to network, learn, and boost your career in a fun and supportive environment.

This is determined by a point system, where you earn points for attending general meetings, website meetings, Girl Code events, the KU hackathon, and more. The Vice President tracks the points, and at the end of the year, the 15 members with the most points are chosen to go.

Yes, as long as you're one of the top 15 members each year, you can be chosen to attend multiple times!

Everything! Your transportation, hotel stay, conference registration, and meals are all taken care of.
